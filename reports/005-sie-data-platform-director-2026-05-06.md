This clears 3.0 only because the comp and Director level are compelling — it does not clear the 3.5 threshold.

**Exception path:** If Victor has hands-on data platform experience not reflected in his resume (past projects, side work, internal tools at AT&T), it's worth a conversation. Otherwise, pass.
